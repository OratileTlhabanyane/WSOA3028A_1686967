This is a bonus blog post showcasing my creative side about how i feel the internet has affected me and I am going to be doing this through a poem called DECOMPOSE

Let the sky open and swallow my perishing sanity

Heaven gates open and accept my so-called serenity

Today marks the day my heartbeat got silenced

The day my toasted golden skin

Slowly deterioated from my unborn flesh

The day my marine blue artiries silently cracked opening

The day my artiries tasted the cold-iced air

And started transitioning to a ruby red colour that none can compared

The day i decomposed

Nothing can compare to the freedom felt by my blood

As it busted through the imprisonment of my veins

The impurities carried by my veins could not sin no more

My heart could not fake tranquility anymore

My snow-crystiled bones could not take it no more

The day i decomposed

I cannot blame you for my weakness

But you are the main reason for my sickness

Crucified and left me so defenseless

The day i decomposed

Thoughts were tired of thinking

Emotions were tired of feeling

Life was tired of living

Heaven was tired of waiting

So

I decomposed
