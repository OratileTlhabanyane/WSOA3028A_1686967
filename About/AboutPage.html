About Page

This blog shows the life of a student as she explores the wonders of the internet and uses her poetry to do so.

The website is going to contain theory blogs, poetry diaries, character blogs and many more.

Black Diamond is a blogger and a vlogger. She writes blogs that relate to the changes of technology today and how they affect her life as a student and also her life as a black woman living in South Africa
